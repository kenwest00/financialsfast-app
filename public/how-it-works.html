Tell us about your business

Before we look at a single transaction, we need context. A guided questionnaire walks you through your business structure, revenue sources, major expenses, and spending patterns. This context is what makes our classification accurate — we're not guessing, we're matching transactions to information you've already told us.

  • Business basics — your name, entity type, industry, and how long you've been operating.
  • Revenue sources — where your income comes from (Stripe, Square, invoices, cash) so we know what deposits to count as revenue.
  • Named expenses — your landlord, software subscriptions, ad platforms, payroll service, and suppliers so we can match them exactly.
  • Personal vs. business — whether this is a dedicated business account or mixed use, and which merchants are personal.

Upload your bank statements

Log into your bank's website, download your monthly statements as PDFs, and drag them into our upload area. Most banks offer statement downloads going back 12+ months. We accept 3 to 12 months of statements depending on what your loan requires.

  • Any U.S. bank — Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, credit unions, online banks. We handle format differences automatically.
  • No bank login required — we never connect to your bank. You download PDFs yourself and upload them here. No Plaid, no third-party access.
  • Instant parsing — our AI reads each statement, extracts every transaction, and confirms what it found: "We identified 847 transactions from January to December 2025."

Review the flagged items

Our four-layer classification system handles the vast majority of transactions automatically. For the small percentage it's unsure about, you'll see two simple groups to review.

"Business or Personal?" — Transactions at merchants that could go either way (Costco, Amazon, gas stations). Just tap business or personal for each one, or mark all of a merchant's transactions at once.

"Tell Us More" — A handful of transactions our AI couldn't categorize with confidence. Pick the right category from a dropdown, or tell us it's personal. Most users have fewer than 10 items in this group.

  • Batch actions — "Mark all Costco as personal" handles multiple transactions with one tap.
  • Transparent confidence — every transaction shows its confidence level so you know what was automatically matched vs. AI-classified.

Download your P&L

Once you've reviewed the flagged items, your complete Profit & Loss statement is generated as a professional PDF. It's formatted for loan applications with clear section hierarchy, proper financial notation, and detailed supporting documentation.

  • Multi-step income statement — Revenue, COGS, Gross Profit, Operating Expenses, Operating Income, Net Income.
  • Memo items — Owner draws, loan principal, CAPEX, and personal expenses shown separately so your lender sees the full picture.
  • Notes & methodology — Every assumption documented: vehicle proration, loan splits, depreciation estimates, mixed-account handling.
  • Transaction detail appendix — Every classified transaction listed by category with date, description, amount, and confidence level.
