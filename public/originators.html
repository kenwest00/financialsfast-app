The Problem

The P&L bottleneck kills deals

You've qualified the borrower. The property works. The lender is ready. Then they ask for a Profit & Loss statement, and everything stops.

⏳

2–4 week delay

Borrower has to find a CPA, schedule an appointment, gather documents, and wait for delivery. Meanwhile, the rate lock expires and competing offers move forward.

💸

$400–$2,000 CPA cost

For a small business owner already stretching to close a loan, an unexpected $400–$2,000 CPA bill creates friction. Some borrowers drop out entirely.

🔄

You can't control it

The borrower's relationship with their CPA is outside your pipeline. You're following up, nudging, and hoping — instead of closing.

The Solution

Give your borrower a link. Get a P&L back today.

Financials Fast is an AI-powered tool that generates professional Profit & Loss statements from bank statement PDFs. Your borrower uploads 3–12 months of statements, answers a short questionnaire, reviews a few flagged transactions, and downloads a formatted P&L — all in about 15 minutes.

The output follows standard income statement formatting with detailed notes and methodology. It's designed to meet lender requirements for owner-prepared financial statements.
