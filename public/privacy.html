Privacy Policy

We built Financials Fast around a simple principle: your financial data should never live on our servers. Here's exactly how that works.

Effective: February 26, 2026

The Short Version

We process your financial data to generate your P&L statement. We do not store, retain, or have access to your financial data after your session ends. There is no database. There are no stored files. There is nothing to breach.

This isn't just a policy — it's how the system is architected. Our servers are stateless. They process your data in memory, return results to your browser, and terminate. The data ceases to exist on our infrastructure when processing is complete.

How Your Data Flows

Step 1: Text Extraction (Your Browser)

When you upload bank statement PDFs, the text is extracted entirely within your browser using a client-side library. The raw PDF files are never uploaded to our servers. Only the extracted text content is transmitted for processing.

Step 2: Transaction Parsing (Our Server)

The extracted text is sent to our server over an encrypted (TLS) connection. Our server forwards this text to an AI language model (Claude, provided by Anthropic) to parse it into structured transaction data. The server receives the structured data, returns it to your browser, and terminates. No data is written to disk, to a database, or to any persistent storage.

Step 3: Classification (Your Browser + Our Server)

Most transactions are classified in your browser using rules based on your questionnaire answers. Transactions that can't be classified locally are sent to our server in small batches for AI-assisted classification. The same ephemeral processing applies — data is processed in memory and discarded after the response is sent.

Step 4: PDF Generation (Our Server)

Your final P&L data is sent to our server to generate a professionally formatted PDF. The PDF is rendered in memory, returned to your browser as a file download, and the data is discarded. The PDF exists only on your device.

Third-Party AI Processing

We use Anthropic's Claude API to parse bank statement text and classify transactions. The following applies to this processing:

  • Only transaction descriptions, amounts, dates, and directions are sent to the AI. Account numbers, routing numbers, and balance information are stripped before transmission.
  • Anthropic's API operates under a zero-data-retention agreement. Your financial data is not stored, logged, or used to train AI models.
  • All data is transmitted over encrypted (TLS) connections.
  • We do not send your name, address, Social Security number, or any other personally identifying information to the AI.

Payment Processing

Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We never receive, process, or store your credit card number, CVV, or billing details. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security certification. Stripe retains your email address and payment details under their own privacy policy, which you can review at stripe.com/privacy.

Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. The only client-side storage we use is:

  • IndexedDB — Stores your questionnaire responses and session state in your browser so you can resume if you navigate away. This data never leaves your browser and is cleared when you complete your session or clear your browser data.
  • Stripe session cookie — A temporary cookie used by Stripe during payment processing. It is set by Stripe, not by us, and expires when payment is complete.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • We do not use your financial data to train AI models.
  • We do not build profiles of our users.
  • We do not use analytics services that track individual users.
  • We do not use data aggregators such as Plaid, Yodlee, or similar services.
  • We do not retain your financial data after your session ends — in any form, on any system we control.

Data Security

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses TLS (HTTPS) encryption.
  • No data at rest: Because we don't store your data, there is no data to encrypt at rest — and no data to breach.
  • No PII logging: Our server logs record only HTTP status codes and response times. Request and response bodies — which contain your financial data — are never logged.
  • Stateless infrastructure: Our servers are serverless functions that process data in memory and terminate after each request. There is no persistent server with access to prior requests.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your personal information, and opt out of the sale of your personal information.

Because we do not store personal financial data, collect minimal personal information (only what Stripe collects for payment processing), and never sell personal information, most CCPA rights are satisfied by our architecture by default. Children's Privacy

Financials Fast is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly process data from minors.

Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
